## How to Use This Document

**For Knowledge Base Building:** Each question maps to a section of extraction. The "Result/Purpose" explains what gets extracted. The "Impact on Process" explains how that piece shapes the final knowledge base.

**For Process Documentation:** Step through each section and collect the answers. The impact column tells you how to integrate each answer into the documentation.

**For Positioning & Sales:** Questions 1-20 (Router + Steps 1-3) provide the core positioning framework. Questions 21-29 (Step 4) provide pricing strategy. Questions 30-36 (Step 5) provide voice and tone guidance. Questions 37-44 (Step 6) provide proof assets.

**For Operational Documentation:** Questions 45-81 (Steps 7-10) provide the operational and logistical foundation for scaling the methodology.

**For Future Evolution:** Question 56-57 (Step 8) signal when the knowledge base needs updating and how the methodology might evolve.
